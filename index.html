Random Number

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to random numbers. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The valule of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time a new game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
